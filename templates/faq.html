FAQ

How do I create an account?

1. On the splash page, click “Create Account”.

2. Enter a valid email address and a secure password.

3. Enter your password again to confirm.

4. Click “Create”.

How do I search for a specific stock?

1. Login, then from the home page, click on the "Market" icon in the top menu (looks like a graph).

2. Click on "Search" to display the search screen.

3. Type the name of the desired stock in the search bar.

How can I buy/save a stock from the top 100?

1. Login, then from the home page, click on the "Market" icon in the top menu (looks like a graph).

2. Click "Top 100" to display the NASDAQ 100 Companies.

3. In the desired stock, set the desired quantity of shares.

4. Click "Buy" or "Save". If successful, the investment will appear in your portfolio on the "Home" page.

How can I deposit/withdraw funds?

1. Login, then go to the “Home” page.

2. Under your balance tracker, enter the amount of funds you want to withdraw/deposit.

3. Click “Increment Balance” to deposit. Click “Decrement Balance” to withdraw.
